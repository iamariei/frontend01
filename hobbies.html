Hello, I’m Ionuț.

A Romanian in the middle of the ocean

Traveling

Be it just in an new neighbourhood or halfway across the globe, I love wandering around and discovering new sights and cultures. So far, I have been to about 20 countries around Europe, North America, and Asia.

One of my favorite trips has been to Kazakhstan's capital, Astana, where I visited a friend from college. The city had amazing brand-new buildings, and the people were some of the friendliest I had ever met.

Another memorable trip has been the one to Iceland. The nature seemed out of this world, and the relaxed atmosphere of the small towns made me fall in love with the country and convinced me to move there in 2017!

In the future, I hope to visit Japan, Greenland, and the Caribbean island of Sint Maarten.

Photography

At the age of 12 I got my first camera, and ever since, I have been fascinated by the beautiful world of photography. I am especially interested in capturing landscapes and cityscapes.

After using increasingly better but bulkier camera gear over time, I have decided to turn back to my phone for all my photography needs, both because I have it with me at all times and because I like the challenge of overcoming its limitations.
